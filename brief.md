# Peeer AI Lab — Website Brief

## Direction
B + C: Design studio credibility + practical playbook.

## Positioning
Peeer AI Lab helps small businesses and design/product/brand teams redesign real work with AI workflows, education, and agents.

## Tone
- Less generic AI SaaS
- More design studio / field notes / practical operating manual
- Calm, editorial, useful
- Korean-first
- Human founder-led

## Homepage Structure
1. Hero: AI로 업무를 다시 설계하는 디자인 스튜디오의 실험실
2. What we work on: SME operations, design/product workflows, brand/content systems, AI agents
3. Workflow Notes: practical blog/playbook entries
4. Studies: case-style workflow breakdowns
5. Work with us: diagnosis, workshop, agent build

## Visual Direction
- Off-white editorial background
- Black/charcoal text
- Muted gray borders
- One accent color, not neon AI
- Large typography, generous whitespace
- Cards feel like notes/playbooks, not SaaS feature boxes
